About Me

Hi! I’m David, a self-taught web developer.

I enjoy the process of solving problems, and therefore work hard and move swiftly from one project to the next. But at the same time, a keen eye for detail sometimes keeps me iterating over particularly interesting systems/solutions (continuous improvement).
The other thing I enjoy besides problem-solving is working in teams. So I encourage you to explore my portfolio and my github pages; You’d surely find something we could collaborate on. Feel free to connect !

My Background

I began programming as a teenager in the Y2K era, and went on to study Computer Science at the undergraduate level few years later. In addition to that, I now hold a postgraduate degree in Aerospace Engineering, as well as Pilot Licences on Airplanes, Helicopters and Drones.

The stay-at-home COVID circumstance of the past year was an opportune moment for re-discovering software development. Now, whenever I’m not busy planning or executing a flight (on duty or at the local flying club), I’m putting some effort into my next development project, or polishing up the current one.
